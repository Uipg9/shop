Shop Mod v1.0.40 - Digital Farm System + Enhanced Loan System

NEW FEATURES:
- Digital Farm System: Purchase and manage automated farms for continuous resource generation
  * 7 Different farm types: Crop, Tree, Fish, Iron, Animal, Mob, and Enchant farms
  * Purchase farms with money, pay daily salaries, collect produced resources
  * Progressive farm levels unlock more advanced farm types
  * Independent from village system - direct player ownership
- Enhanced Housing: Houses now provide +10 worker slots (up from +1)
- Quick Start: Base worker capacity increased from 3 to 10 slots
- /farm command: Access the new Digital Farm System GUI
- Smart Loan Payments: Loans now auto-withdraw from bank investments when wallet is insufficient
  * Daily loan payments automatically draw from bank if needed
  * Manual loan payments also support auto-withdrawal
  * /loan pay <amount> and /loan pay (full) commands added

FARM TYPES:
Level 1 (Basic):
- Crop Farm: $5,000 | $200/day salary | Produces 10 food daily
- Tree Farm: $8,000 | $400/day salary | Produces 8 wood daily
- Fish Farm: $12,000 | $600/day salary | Produces 6 food daily

Level 2 (Advanced):
- Iron Farm: $15,000 | $800/day salary | Produces 5 ore daily
- Animal Farm: $18,000 | $1000/day salary | Produces 4 food daily

Level 3 (Expert):
- Mob Farm: $25,000 | $1200/day salary | Produces 3 rare items daily
- Enchant Farm: $30,000 | $1500/day salary | Produces 2 rare items daily

IMPROVEMENTS:
- Village housing capacity dramatically increased (10x multiplier)
- Players can now hire 10 villagers immediately without building houses
- Faster early game progression with immediate access to large workforce
- Separate automation system for players who prefer direct resource management

TECHNICAL:
- Added complete farm management system with purchase, toggle, and collection mechanics
- Integrated daily production processing with automatic salary deduction
- New farm GUI with tabbed interface for easy management
- Farm progression system with level requirements for advanced farms

Compatible with Minecraft 1.21.11 + Fabric Loader 0.18.4
