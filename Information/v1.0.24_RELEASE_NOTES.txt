NEW FEATURES:
==============

1. DIGITAL VILLAGE SYSTEM (/village)
   - Hire up to 10 digital villagers to generate passive income
   - Each worker type has different costs, salaries, and income
   - Workers can be upgraded to earn more (max level 50)
   - Daily income minus salaries = your profit
   - Fire workers anytime for 50% refund

   WORKER TYPES:
   -------------
   FARMER
   - Hire Cost: $250
   - Daily Salary: $100
   - Daily Income: $150
   - Net Profit: $50/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.0x per level
   
   LUMBERJACK
   - Hire Cost: $200
   - Daily Salary: $80
   - Daily Income: $120
   - Net Profit: $40/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.0x per level
   
   FISHERMAN
   - Hire Cost: $180
   - Daily Salary: $70
   - Daily Income: $110
   - Net Profit: $40/day
   - Income Multiplier: 0.9x per level
   
   RANCHER
   - Hire Cost: $300
   - Daily Salary: $120
   - Daily Income: $180
   - Net Profit: $60/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.1x per level
   
   BLACKSMITH
   - Hire Cost: $400
   - Daily Salary: $150
   - Daily Income: $220
   - Net Profit: $70/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.15x per level
   
   MINER
   - Hire Cost: $500
   - Daily Salary: $200
   - Daily Income: $300
   - Net Profit: $100/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.2x per level
   
   MERCHANT
   - Hire Cost: $600
   - Daily Salary: $180
   - Daily Income: $280
   - Net Profit: $100/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.3x per level
   
   ENCHANTER (Best profit!)
   - Hire Cost: $1,000
   - Daily Salary: $250
   - Daily Income: $400
   - Net Profit: $150/day
   - Income Multiplier: 1.5x per level

   HOW TO USE:
   -----------
   - Use /village to open village GUI
   - LEFT CLICK worker icon to hire
   - SHIFT+LEFT CLICK to upgrade worker level
   - RIGHT CLICK to fire worker (50% refund)
   - Upgrade cost = Hire cost × (current level + 1)
   - Profits paid automatically every Minecraft day at dawn
   - Max 10 workers total

2. BANK STORAGE UPGRADES
   - Expand bank storage from 27 to 54 slots
   - 4 upgrade levels available
   
   UPGRADE COSTS:
   --------------
   Level 0 → 1: $5,000 (27 → 36 slots)
   Level 1 → 2: $15,000 (36 → 45 slots)
   Level 2 → 3: $50,000 (45 → 54 slots)
   
   HOW TO USE:
   -----------
   - Open /bank
   - Click the chest icon in center
   - Purchase upgrades to unlock more storage
   - Locked slots shown as gray glass panes

EXISTING FEATURES:
==================
- Loan System (/loan) - Credit score based loans with daily payments
- Teleportation (/sethome, /home) - Set and teleport to home location
- Night Vision Toggle (/nv) - Turn night vision on/off
- Shop System (/shop) - 500+ items across 5 tiers
- Bank Investment - Risky daily returns based on difficulty
- Wallet Interest - 10% guaranteed daily interest
- Upgrades (/upgrades) - Health, Flight, Luck, Regeneration, etc.

COMMANDS:
=========
/village - Open digital village management
/bank - Open bank for storage and investments
/loan - Manage loans (status, calculator, take, delay)
/shop - Open item shop
/sethome - Set home location
/home - Teleport home
/nv - Toggle night vision
/upgrades - View/purchase upgrades
/balance - Check wallet balance
/sell - Sell items in hand

TIPS:
=====
- Start with cheap workers (Fisherman, Lumberjack, Farmer)
- Upgrade workers to increase income via multipliers
- Enchanter has best profit but highest cost
- Upgrade bank storage early for more item storage
- Use bank investments to build credit score for better loans
- Set your home before exploring far from spawn
